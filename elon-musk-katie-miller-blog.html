Simulation Theory: Our One Job

This is where the conversation gets truly fascinating. Musk has a fully developed theory about why simulation theory makes perfect sense‚Äîand what it means for humanity:

"The most interesting outcome is the most likely. If simulation theory is accurate that makes perfect sense, because if someone is running a wide range of simulated futures they're going to stop the simulation when it gets boring."

How SpaceX Tests This Daily

When SpaceX or Tesla runs simulations to understand how a car, robot, or spaceship will work, we run thousands of them on the computer. The simulations we actually pay attention to are the most interesting ones. The simulation where everything goes perfectly on the rocket? We don't really look at that; it's fine, but boring... So we hunt for the envelope of flight paths where the rocket can actually make it to orbit without blowing up, we find those boundaries, and then when we launch the real rocket we do everything possible to stay inside them.
